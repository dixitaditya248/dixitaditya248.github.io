"Bringing deep learning to the deep spectrum"

Hello and welcome!

I'm Aditya Dixit, a Ph.D. scholar in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Indore, where I explore the fascinating world of Hyperspectral Imaging under the guidance of Dr. Puneet Gupta. My research blends Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision to unlock new possibilities in this field, pushing the boundaries of how we analyze and interpret complex visual data.

My journey in Computer Science began with a Bachelor's degree (B.Tech) from Government Engineering College Jhalawar, Rajasthan, India, which I completed in 2020. Over the years, my passion for Deep Learning, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision has only deepened. Beyond research, I thrive on coding, developing projects, and engaging with tech communities. I love exchanging ideas, mentoring, and collaborating on innovative solutions. Outside the academic and tech world, I enjoy travelling, exploring new places, and immersing myself in different cultures. In my free time, you’ll find me reading religious texts, watching cricket, playing chess, solving puzzles, or listening to music.

I’m always eager to connect, discuss, and collaborate on exciting research problems. Feel free to reach out—I’d love to hear from you!
