Research overview

Design of robust deep learning models

Techniques to denoise the Hyperspectral Images.

Hyperspectral Images (HSI) contain data from multiple spectrums. It is widely employed in various real-world applications, including remote sensing, agriculture, and marine monitoring. It is depicted as a three-dimensional array with two spatial and one spectral dimension. Unfortunately, noise affects sensing quality due to limited light and air interference. To solve this, we proposed an HSI denoising technique incorporating a 3-D transformer-based encoder for capturing long-range and depthwise dependencies, a 3-D U-Net for structural integrity preservation, and a 3-D CNN-based decoder for successful HSI denoising.
